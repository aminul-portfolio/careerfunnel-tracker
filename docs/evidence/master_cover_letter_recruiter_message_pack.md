**Purpose:** Map the posting to two stated requirements without repeating the full CV.

**Purpose:** Prove domain credibility for analyst roles in FinTech, payments, FX, or operations.

**What to customise:** Company names only if already on CV; keep volumes and systems factual.

In money transfer and FX operations, I worked in AML-aware, customer-verification-led environments and supported multi-currency reconciliation, including an in-house Excel reconciliation system used for daily control. I am comfortable explaining exceptions, reviewing discrepancies, and translating operational questions into structured reporting inputs rather than informal spreadsheets alone.

### 4. Evidence from software support / KPI reporting

**Purpose:** Show analyst-adjacent delivery: training, support, KPI cycles, stakeholder communication.

I also bring software support and KPI reporting experience, including platform support in a large operational network and recurring performance reporting for management review. That background helps me work with business users, clarify data definitions, and deliver reporting that is usable for decisions, not only technically correct.

### 5. Evidence from portfolio projects

**Purpose:** One project only; tie to role type using the Project Selection table below.

### 6. Closing paragraph

**Purpose:** Polite close, availability, CV reference, no pressure.

**What to customise:** Optional sentence on start date or location if relevant.

**Copy/paste wording:**

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my operations, reconciliation, and analytics portfolio experience could support your team. My CV (**Aminul_Islam_Data_Analyst_CV**) and GitHub portfolio are available for review. Thank you for your time and consideration.

Yours sincerely,

Aminul Islam
