## Recruiter Communication Rules

- [ ] Keep first outreach under 140 words.
- [ ] Lead with role fit, not project volume.
- [ ] Mention one domain strength only.
- [ ] Mention one or two projects only.
- [ ] Attach or reference `Aminul_Islam_Data_Analyst_CV`.
- [ ] Mention UK work authorisation when useful.
- [ ] Do not say LinkedIn profile has been updated.
- [ ] Do not claim proprietary BI platform artefacts unless verified.
- [ ] Do not claim production SQL project delivery unless evidenced.
- [ ] Do not claim external AI/API implementation.
- [ ] Do not claim scraping, auto-apply, Gmail, Calendar, live SaaS, production users, or background automation.
- [ ] Keep the message aligned with the actual job type.
