I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my operations experience and analytics portfolio could support your team. Please find my CV (**Aminul_Islam_Data_Analyst_CV**) attached. Thank you for considering my application.

I bring strong Excel reporting capability, including an in-house reconciliation system, Power Query, DAX-oriented workbook logic, pivot tables, and VBA-supported controls. My experience covers reconciliation, operational reporting, and exception review in regulated financial environments.

### When the role asks for Python

I maintain a Python/Django analytics portfolio on GitHub, using Pandas/NumPy skills in analyst workflows. SQL is a skill I am strengthening alongside Python and Excel analytics. I can apply joins, aggregations, filtering, grouping, and reporting-query logic, but I do not claim production SQL project delivery unless separately evidenced for that application.

My current reporting evidence is Excel/Power Query, BI-ready CSV exports, and dashboard-style portfolio work. I am ready to transfer this reporting discipline into Power BI or Tableau where required, but I do not claim a verified production dashboard artefact unless one exists for that application.
