## Cross-Portfolio Claim Boundaries

- **Power BI:** claim portfolio publishing evidence, star-schema modelling, DAX, and Power BI Service portfolio publishing only where the public proof pack supports it. Do not claim production Power BI administration.
- **dbt / DuckDB:** claim dbt Core and DuckDB portfolio analytics-engineering evidence through bakeops-dbt. Do not claim dbt Cloud, Airflow, Snowflake, or production orchestration.
- **BigQuery:** claim optional dbt validation target with safe credential handling only. Do not claim production warehouse ownership.
- **Docker:** claim local reviewer workflow using Dockerfile and Docker Compose only. Do not claim Docker production deployment.

## Portfolio Claim Safety Rules

- Do not claim live SaaS unless deployment evidence exists.
- Do not claim real users/customers unless proven.
- Do not claim AI/Gmail/Calendar/external automation unless implemented and tested.
- Do not claim CI passed unless verified.
- Do not claim local validation complete unless terminal proof exists.
- Do not claim project-suite integrations are live unless implemented and tested.
